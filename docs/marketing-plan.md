# Marketing Plan

## Positioning

Market Reaction Forecaster is the sharp, consumer-facing answer to "AI for stocks" without pretending to be a broker. The wedge is simple: large-cap tech, explicit calls, and a disciplined event workflow.

## Core message

`Stop drowning in tech-stock headlines. Get explicit buy, hold, and sell calls with evidence, ranges, and profile-aware sizing guidance.`

## Target audience

- active retail investors
- tech-stock enthusiasts
- subscribers to finance newsletters and creator-led market communities
- users who already pay for research, screeners, or charting tools

## Channels

- X / finance Twitter launch threads
- Product Hunt / AI tooling communities
- finance newsletter partnerships
- creator affiliate demos on YouTube and Substack
- comparison content against generic "AI stock picker" tools

## Launch assets

- landing page
- pricing page
- demo GIFs of feed, recommendation detail, and model portfolio
- short founder thread on why the product focuses on large-cap tech only
- compliance-conscious FAQ

## Acquisition hooks

- delayed sample calls on the homepage
- 7-day free trial
- "see how your risk profile changes the recommendation"
- report exports that feel premium and shareable

## Retention hooks

- daily refresh expectations
- event-driven watchlist relevance
- visible profile impact on recommendation sizing
- benchmark and backtest context that reward repeat use

## Launch KPI targets

- landing-page to signup conversion
- signup to disclosure acknowledgment
- trial start to first watchlist creation
- trial to paid conversion
- 30-day retention
