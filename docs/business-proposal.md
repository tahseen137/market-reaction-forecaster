# Business Proposal

## Executive summary

Market Reaction Forecaster is a premium consumer subscription product for self-directed investors who want faster, more structured conviction on large-cap tech stocks. The product translates filings, company news, and event signals into explicit buy, hold, and sell calls with scenario ranges, profile-aware sizing guidance, and a model portfolio.

## Problem

Retail investors face:

- too much fragmented market information
- too little structure around event interpretation
- weak discipline around sizing and follow-through
- low trust in generic "AI stock picker" claims

## Proposed solution

Offer a focused subscription tool that:

- narrows the universe to large-cap tech
- surfaces explicit directional calls
- explains why the call exists
- adapts the presentation to each user's risk profile
- keeps simulated portfolio behavior visible

## Customer

- self-directed US investors
- active tech-stock followers
- users comfortable paying for research software, not a broker

## Positioning

This is not a brokerage and not a generic chatbot. It is a consumer intelligence product for event-driven tech-stock research.

## Monetization

- `Free`
  - delayed sample feed
  - public pages
- `Pro Monthly`
- `$19/month`
  - 7-day trial
- `Pro Annual`
- `$190/year`
  - 7-day trial

## Why it can win

- narrow initial scope instead of whole-market sprawl
- explicit output instead of vague "insights"
- profile-aware output instead of one-size-fits-all signals
- strong UX around evidence, ranges, and invalidation

## Risks

- compliance and perception risk from explicit recommendations
- churn risk if calls feel too generic
- vendor fragility from low-cost market data
- support burden from consumer billing and expectations

## Mitigations

- keep disclosure language visible everywhere
- avoid guarantees and brokerage execution
- keep model version, benchmark, and backtest metadata attached
- limit the first universe to a sector users already obsess over

## 12-month objective

Reach a repeatable subscription funnel in one narrow market segment before expanding into broader sector coverage or more advanced portfolio tooling.
