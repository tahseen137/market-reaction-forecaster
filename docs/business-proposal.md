# Business Proposal

## Executive summary

Market Reaction Forecaster is a research intelligence platform for teams that need faster, more structured judgment about market-moving events. It combines event ingestion, scenario simulation, forecast calibration, and paper portfolio tracking into one workflow.

The initial commercial wedge is not retail investing. It is specialized research teams and small institutional users who need better event interpretation without building a large internal quant and data platform.

## Problem

Research teams face three persistent issues:

- too much unstructured information,
- inconsistent analyst judgment,
- and weak feedback loops on whether event calls were actually good.

Traditional dashboards show data. They do not usually show:

- who might react,
- why the market might care,
- what the second-order effects are,
- and how confident the system should be.

## Proposed solution

Provide a workspace that ingests market events, simulates stakeholder and market responses, scores directional outcomes across horizons, and tracks whether those forecasts hold up in backtests and paper portfolios.

## Ideal customer profile

- sector-focused hedge funds
- family offices with research staff
- independent research boutiques
- investor relations and strategy teams at public companies

## Buyer and champion

- Buyer: CIO, Head of Research, or Head of Strategy
- Champion: senior analyst, PM, strategist, or quant researcher

## Value proposition

- Faster event-to-thesis workflow
- Better consistency across analysts
- Clearer evidence trails for investment committee review
- Safer experimentation through paper trading before capital deployment

## Why now

- Markets react faster to information than manual teams can process.
- LLMs make event summarization and scenario generation dramatically easier.
- Smaller funds increasingly need institutional-quality tooling without institutional-size engineering teams.

## MVP packaging

### Package 1: Research Pilot

- 5 named users
- 3 watchlists
- manual event ingestion
- backtest summary dashboard
- paper portfolio

Suggested price: USD 3,000 to 6,000 per month

### Package 2: Team Pro

- 20 named users
- automated ingestion
- custom coverage universe
- analyst notes and approvals
- export APIs

Suggested price: USD 8,000 to 15,000 per month

### Package 3: Enterprise

- private deployment
- SSO
- audit logs
- premium data connector support
- dedicated onboarding and support

Suggested price: USD 30,000+ annually plus data pass-through and services

## Go-to-market plan

### Wedge

Start with one event-heavy vertical such as semiconductors, energy, or biotech.

### Entry motion

- Design partner pilots with small funds and research shops
- Event replay workshops using their own historical calls
- White-glove onboarding to prove value fast

### Early proof points

- forecast calibration on unseen historical periods
- reduced analyst time per event
- paper portfolio performance versus benchmark
- repeat usage in investment committee prep

## Competitive landscape

This market is crowded around:

- news analytics,
- event detection,
- transcript search,
- and sentiment feeds.

Differentiation comes from combining:

- second-order reaction simulation,
- confidence calibration,
- historical replay,
- and workflow-level feedback loops.

## Risks

- crowded category with strong incumbents
- data licensing costs
- compliance and marketing language risk
- risk of users over-trusting narrative forecasts

## Mitigations

- start in a narrow domain with better calibration
- stay on the research-support side first
- make uncertainty visible everywhere
- focus on explainability and replay, not hype
