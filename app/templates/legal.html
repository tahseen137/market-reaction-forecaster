Disclosures

Research, not guaranteed outcomes.

Market Reaction Forecaster is a software product that generates model-driven buy, hold, and sell calls. It does not execute trades, custody assets, or guarantee performance.

How to use it

  • Use the product as a structured research input.
  • Review evidence links, event context, and analog sample size before acting.
  • Understand that historical simulations are not promises of future returns.

What the product does not do

  • No trade placement or brokerage execution.
  • No guarantee that any recommendation will be profitable.
  • No claim that model output is suitable for every investor.

Market data limitations

Some views may use delayed or sampled data depending on entitlement state and data-provider availability. Recommendation snapshots include model version, benchmark, and evidence status so you can see exactly what informed the output.
